# VelociKeys AI — Privacy & Data Safety

This site hosts the public-facing legal documents for the VelociKeys AI
Android keyboard. The Google Play Store listing links here for both the
privacy policy and the data-safety form mapping.

## Documents

- **[Privacy Policy](privacy.html)** — what data the keyboard processes,
  where it lives, who can see it, and what controls users have.
- **[Data Safety form mapping](data-safety.html)** — the exact answers
  submitted to the Play Console Data Safety questionnaire.
